What It Is & Where It Came From

What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is simply dummy text used in the printing and typesetting industry. Designers and developers use it as placeholder text when creating websites, books, or layouts. It helps focus on design and structure without being distracted by real content.

Where Did It Come From?

Lorem Ipsum comes from a classical Latin text written by Cicero in 45 BC. The original text is from a work called "De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum", which means "On the Ends of Good and Evil."

Over time, parts of the original Latin text were scrambled and altered to create the placeholder text we use today.

Why Do We Use It?

  • To fill space in design mockups
  • To test layouts and fonts
  • To simulate real text without meaning
  • To focus on visual design instead of content
